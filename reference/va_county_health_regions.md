# Virginia county and Health Planning Region crosswalk

A reference dataset of 133 counties and independent cities in Virginia
and their associated health planning geographies (Health Planning
District, Health Planning Region, and Metropolitan Statistical Area
(MSA) Rating Area).

## Usage

``` r
va_county_health_regions
```

## Format

### `va_county_health_regions`

- County:

  Short name of the county or independent city.

- County_full_name:

  Full official name of the county or independent city.

- Health_Planning_District:

  Virginia Health Planning District.

- Health_Planning_Region:

  Virginia Health Planning Region.

- MSA_Rating_Area:

  Metropolitan Statistical Area (Rating Area).
